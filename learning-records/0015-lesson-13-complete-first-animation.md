# Lesson 13 complete: first Timeline animation

Completed 2026-07-06 (completion requested by the learner). The learner made the first animation
artifact for the kitchen asset pack: a small tomato bounce loop built with Blockbench's Animate mode,
Timeline, and three position keyframes.

## What is now established

- A keyframe is a saved pose or value at a specific time.
- A simple loop can be built from three position keyframes: down, up, down.
- Grouping the moving tomato parts before animating keeps the whole prop moving together.
- The first and last keyframes should match when the motion needs to loop cleanly.
- The daily itch.io posting streak now has a Day-13 private draft ready for review.
