# Lesson 2 complete: combining cubes into a prop

Completed 2026-06-24 (self-reported by the learner). The learner built and rendered a chopping
board with a knife, saved the editable Blockbench model, and completed the second daily lesson.

## What is now established

- Multiple cubes can be resized and positioned to read as one recognizable prop.
- Parts can be organized and named in the Outliner (`board`, `handle`, and `blade`).
- Simple material colors and proportions distinguish wood, a knife handle, and a steel blade.
- The Day-1 workflow has been repeated: model → texture/color → frame → render → post.
