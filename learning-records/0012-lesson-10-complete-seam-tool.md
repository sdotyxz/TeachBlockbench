# Lesson 10 complete: Seam Tool sauce bottle

Completed 2026-07-02 (completion requested by the learner). The learner built a
small sauce bottle and practiced the Seam Tool's beginner-safe mesh workflow:
one hidden back edge became a Divide seam before generating and painting the
texture template.

## What is now established

- Seam Tool works on mesh edges, not ordinary cube elements.
- A seam is a planned UV cut, not a painted line on the model.
- One hidden Divide seam can make a cylinder texture layout easier to paint.
- The front label should be placed away from the back texture cut.
- The daily itch.io posting streak now has a Day-10 private draft ready for review.
