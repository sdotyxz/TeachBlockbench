# Starting point: total beginner, aiming for a sellable asset pack

Established at workspace setup (2026-06-23). The learner has **no prior 3D modeling and no prior
Blockbench experience**, so all 3D/Blockbench terminology is new and must be defined on first use.
The mission is a **shareable Overcooked-style asset pack on itch.io**, pursued through a **daily
postable win**, with an explicit preference for the *fastest path to a tangible result* over
completeness. Pure Blockbench — no game engine. See [[MISSION.md]].

**Implications for future sessions:**
- Zone-of-proximal-development floor is zero: Lesson 1 starts at "open the app, make one cube."
- Hold back UV mapping, rigging, and export formats until the cube → move/resize → color loop is
  fluent.
- Keep every lesson to one ~1-hour sitting that ends with something the learner can post.
