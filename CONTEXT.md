# TeachBlockbench Course

TeachBlockbench is a beginner Blockbench course for building a postable Overcooked-style asset pack
one focused daily lesson at a time.

## Language

**New-skill lesson**:
A daily lesson whose main learning goal introduces a genuinely new Blockbench skill, workflow, or
asset-pack concept instead of asking the learner to repeat yesterday's core move on a different prop.
_Avoid_: repeat lesson, practice-only lesson

**Postable artifact**:
The concrete visual output of a lesson that is good enough to share publicly that day, usually a
render, screenshot, before/after image, or short progress post.
_Avoid_: exercise result, homework

**UV mapping**:
The Blockbench skill of deciding which part of a texture image appears on each face of a model.
_Avoid_: texture drawing, painting pixels
